ABOUT US

What are we?

Speed Admirer is a community and brand born from a deep passion for cars, speed, and innovation. Since the very beginning, we have focused on combining style, performance, and passion in everything we create. With the philosophy "Elegance in Speed", we don’t just represent an automotive brand — we represent an identity for those who truly admire speed.

HISTORY

1990 – Speed Admirer was founded in Jakarta by a small group of car enthusiasts who shared a love for classic sports cars. What started as casual monthly gatherings quickly turned into a movement.

1995 – The community expanded and began organizing legal racing events at circuits, officially introducing the first Speed Admirer logo.

2005 – Speed Admirer launched its creative division, focusing on design, merchandise, and automotive branding.

2015 – A new, modern logo was introduced to reflect the brand’s evolution into a more elegant and minimalist style.

2023 – Speed Admirer entered the digital world, producing automotive content, international collaborations, and premium merchandise that gained strong recognition worldwide.
